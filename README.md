# Real Estate Sales Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview  
This project showcases an **end-to-end business intelligence solution** built in Tableau to analyze real estate sales data and uncover actionable insights.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor **sales performance, project profitability, cancellations, and sales team efficiency**, helping drive data-informed decision-making.

---

## Business Problem  

The organization is experiencing a **decline in sales performance**, along with **high cancellations and inconsistent project outcomes**.

### Key Challenges:
- Declining **Net Sales & Units (YoY)**  
- High **cancellations and adjustments impacting revenue**  
- Uneven performance across projects  
- Inconsistent productivity across sales teams  
- Lack of visibility into geographical and seasonal trends  

---

## Key Business Questions  

- What is causing the **decline in sales performance**?  
- Which projects are **driving revenue vs generating losses**?  
- Where are we losing revenue due to **cancellations and adjustments**?  
- Who are the **top and bottom performing sales reps**?  
- Are there **time-based or regional trends** affecting sales?  

---

## Solution Approach  

- Built **interactive dashboards in Tableau** to provide multi-level analysis  
- Performed **data cleaning and transformation** using Excel/SAP  
- Designed **KPI-driven visualizations** for quick decision-making  
- Applied **Pareto analysis** to identify key revenue contributors  
- Enabled **drill-down capabilities** across projects, teams, and time  

---

## Dashboard Features  

### Overview Dashboard  
- KPI tracking: Net Sales, Gross Sales, Units, Adjustments, Cancellations  
- Year-over-Year comparison  
- Pareto analysis of business entities  
- Sales distribution by project and geography  

### Project Performance  
- Comparison of Gross vs Net Sales vs Adjustments  
- Identification of **loss-making projects**  
- Unit and cancellation analysis  
- Inventory status (Pre-selling, RFO, RHomes)  

### Sales Team Performance  
- Performance tracking for:
  - Sales Directors (SD)  
  - Sales Managers (SM)  
  - Account Managers (AM)  
- Metrics: Gross Sales & Net Sales  
- Identification of top and underperforming reps  

### Sales & Trends Analysis  
- Monthly trends for Sales, Units, Adjustments  
- Cumulative vs Monthly performance  
- Flagship vs Core project comparison  
- Seasonal pattern identification  

---

## Data Storytelling (Key Insights)  

- **Net Sales dropped by 45.1% YoY**, indicating major revenue decline  
- **80–90% of revenue comes from a few projects** (Pareto effect)  
- Several projects show **high adjustments and cancellations**, reducing profitability  
- Sales performance is **skewed—few top performers drive most revenue**  
- Clear **seasonal trends** observed in sales and unit movement  

---

## Business Impact  

This dashboard helps stakeholders to:  
- Identify **revenue leakage** and reduce cancellations  
- Focus on **high-performing projects**  
- Improve **sales team productivity**  
- Optimize **inventory and sales strategy**  
- Make faster, **data-driven decisions**  

---

## Recommendations  

- Scale strategies from **top-performing projects**  
- Reduce cancellations through **better customer engagement & process improvements**  
- Train or restructure **low-performing sales teams**  
- Align inventory across **Pre-selling and Ready-to-Move units**  
- Use seasonal insights for **campaign planning and forecasting**
